Welcome!

This website is heavily inspired by the sporcle quiz "Flags of the World".

My friends and I play it a lot but I have always had a few issues with it. I always felt the images were too small and it wasnt very beginer friendly so I set out to fix those issues here!

The ways I went about achiving these goals were...

1. Making the images much larger (and higher quality) (and have the correct colors) I got the images of the flags from here!

2. Making an easy mode where players can learn the most recognizable flags (the flags I had the easiest time learning at least), and then a medium mode where players are thrown into learning all the flags, but just 20 at a time to make it more managable.
